Instructions

Tic-Tac-Toe is a game that you presumably already know how to play.
It's a really simple game, right?

Most people believe this. However, if you actually think about it,
you'll find that Tic-Tac-Toe isn't as simple as you believe!

Tic-Tac-Toe (like a number of other games) requires you to look ahead and predict
what the person playing against you will do next.
